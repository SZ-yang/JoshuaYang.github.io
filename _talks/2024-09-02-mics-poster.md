Poster presentation on small area estimation techniques applied to MICS survey data to derive subnational estimates of critical child health indicators including neonatal mortality rate, vaccination coverage, and stunting across Sierra Leone, Gambia, and Laos. Delivered by Jon Wakefield.

Co-authors: Dong, Q., Wu, Y., Boskovic, A., Yang, J., Yu, Z., Carvajal Velez, L., Gashi, N., Kauv, S., Bao, M., Jiang, J., Li, R., & Wakefield, J.
